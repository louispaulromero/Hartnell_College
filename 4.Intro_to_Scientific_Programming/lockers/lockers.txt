Lockers
You have 1000 lockers and 1000 students

All lockers are initiall locked.

First student opens every locker

2nd student closes every other locker

3rd student opens(if closed) or closes(if opened) every 3rd locker

4th student opens(if closed) or closes(if opened) every 4th locker

...

999th student opens(if closed) or closes(if opened) every 999th locker

1000th student opens(if closed) or closes(if opened) every 1000th locker

====

Write a program to find out which exact lockers are open, and how many lockers total are open.

Hint: There are 32 lockers open.
